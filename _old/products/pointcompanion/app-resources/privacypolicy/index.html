Privacy Policy | Point Companion

This privacy policy's aim is to be as transparent as possible about what this application will do with the information on your phone when used (either directly on indirectly) and the data stored with the Point services that the application will be interacting with.

Effective as of 5th March 2017

Personal Data On Your Phone

This application will not access any personal data that is on your phone.

Caching of data on your phone

This application will potentially cache the last 10 events from your timeline, including user and device identifiers. This is used soley to improve live tile behaviour.

Google Analytics

Google Analytics is an analysis service provided by Google. Google utilizes the data collected to track and examine the use of Point Companion and to prepare reports on its activities. This information may be shared with other Google services. Google may use the data collected to contextualize and personalize the ads of its own advertising network.

This application uses this collected information to determine how its users use the app. This information is used, but not limited to, the following:

  • Determine high level view of user base for the application (e.g. total number of active users)

  • Navigation trends within the application

  • Setting trends (e.g. which settings are disabled/enabled by users) within the application

The full privacy policy of Google Analytics can be found at their website.

Advertisements

This application will monetize via the inclusion of advertisements. By using the application, you agree to any data that may be provided to the advertisement suppliers, which may include the tracking of your uses across applications.

Minut

By using this application, you will be authorising read/write access to your data stored on Minuts servers. You are also agreeing to their privacy policy which can be found at their website.
